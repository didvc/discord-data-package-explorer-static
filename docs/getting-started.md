# Getting Started

## 1. Request your Discord Data Package

1. Open Discord (desktop or web).
2. Go to **User Settings → Privacy & Safety**.
3. Click **Request Data**.
4. Make sure **Messages** and **Account Info** are included.
5. Wait for the email (can take several hours to days).

## 2. Download and use

1. Download the `package.zip` from the email.
2. Go to the [live app](https://didvc.github.io/discord-data-package-explorer-static/).
3. Drag and drop the zip file (or click to select).

That's it — everything runs in your browser.

## Notes

- The tool only needs the `messages/` and account files.
- Large packages may take some time to parse.
- No data ever leaves your device.
